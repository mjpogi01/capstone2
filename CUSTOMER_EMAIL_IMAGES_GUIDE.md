## üí° Why Are Images Blocked?

Gmail blocks images by default to:
- **Protect your privacy** - prevent senders from knowing if you opened the email
- **Block malicious content** - prevent harmful images from loading
- **Save data** - reduce mobile data usage

**Yohanns emails are safe!** We only include our logo and promotional images - no tracking pixels or harmful content.
